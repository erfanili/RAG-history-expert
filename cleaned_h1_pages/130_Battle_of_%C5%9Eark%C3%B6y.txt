The Battle of Sarkoy or Sarkoy operation (Bulgarian: , Turkish: Sarkoy Ckarmas) took place between 9 and 11 February 1913 during the First Balkan War between Bulgaria and the Ottoman Empire. The Ottomans attempted a counter-attack, but were defeated by the Bulgarians at the Battles of Bulair and Sarkoy.
