Dobro Pole or Dobro Polie (Macedonian: ), (Bulgarian: ), (Serbian: ), (Greek: , romanized: Ntompro Pole) is a peak situated on the GreeceNorth Macedonia border.

The nearest villages are Zovik in the Novaci Municipality in the Mariovo region of North Macedonia, and Promachoi in the Voras Mountains in the Pella regional unit, northern Greece.

The World War I Battle of Dobro Pole was fought here from 15 to 18 September 1918.
